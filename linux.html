My linux setup

Here is an incomplete list of softwares and setups on my laptops, which I try to keep track of.

Terms connected by & mean that they are utilised on different laptops. Terms connected by + mean that they are used together on one (or both) laptop(s).

  • Linux distributions: Xubuntu 24.04 & Fedora 41 (i3 spin).
  • Shell: fish & zsh.
    • Having said that, my scripts are always written in bash.
  • Terminal: xfce4-terminal & kitty.
  • Window manager: i3 + i3status.
  • File manager: Thunar + yazi.
  • Symlink/dotfile manager: GNU Stow.
  • LaTeX distribution: TeX Live.
  • LaTeX editor: Vim 9.1 + VimTeX + UltiSnips.
    • I have learnt quite much from the excellent blogs written by Gilles Castel. It is a great regret that I can never convey my gratitude to him directly.
  • Web browser: Firefox + Vimium + Dark Reader.
  • Email client: Alpine.
    • Since the webmail of Leiden University runs on Outlook, I have to find a client that supports OAuth 2.0. Somehow, Alpine is the simplest one.
  • PDF viewer: zathura.
    • Also for djvu, ps and cb.
  • Media player: mpv.
    • The mpv contained in Fedora’s default repo is not complete, due to the non-freeness of certain codecs. A solution is to install mpv from RPMFusion.
  • Synchronisation: Syncthing.
