# Every subgroup of a finitely generated free abelian group is finitely generated

---

In this article I will show the proof that every subgroup of a free abelian group is finitely generated. The reason why I write this is that I want to test the function of my blog.

The main idea of the proof is that, since abelian group is nothing but $\Z$-module, and $\Z$ is actually Noetherian. We want to show that every finitely generated module over a Noetherian ring is Noetherian.
